## Summary

Meta-analysis of ten paper drafts spanning peer-reviewed conferences, arXiv preprints, workshop papers, and a law review article. All draw from a shared empirical corpus (236 models, 132,416 graded results, 82 attack techniques). Covers CCS main paper, AIES ethics, format-lock, DETECTED_PROCEEDS, polyhedral geometry, VerbosityGuard, defense impossibility, and benchmark specification.
