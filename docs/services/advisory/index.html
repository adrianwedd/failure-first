What We Advise On

Strategic guidance for organizations navigating the intersection of AI safety, regulatory compliance, and commercial deployment. We help translate research findings into defensible safety postures and compliant system architectures.

Advisory Areas

EU AI Act Compliance

Gap analysis between current testing practices and Article 9 requirements for high-risk AI systems. Evidence package development for conformity assessments, technical documentation templates, risk management system design.

  • Compliance gap report with remediation roadmap
  • Technical documentation templates for notified bodies
  • Risk management system architecture review
  • Ongoing regulatory intelligence on implementation guidance

NIST AI RMF Implementation

Practical implementation of NIST AI Risk Management Framework functions (Govern, Map, Measure, Manage) for embodied AI systems. Control selection, evidence gathering strategies, and documentation for government procurement.

  • NIST AI RMF function mapping to system architecture
  • Control implementation guidance by risk category
  • Evidence collection playbook for measurement
  • Government procurement positioning strategy

Insurance Requirements

Translation of research findings into insurer-legible risk metrics. Quantitative evidence packages for underwriting, actuarial risk modeling support, and claims defense documentation strategies.

  • Quantitative risk metrics dashboard for underwriters
  • Pre-incident evidence package preparation
  • Claims defense documentation protocols
  • Insurer presentation materials and technical Q&A prep

Safety Standards Participation

Strategic positioning in emerging safety standards development (ISO, IEEE, BSI). Comment drafting for regulatory consultations, consortium membership guidance, and standards working group representation strategy.

  • Standards landscape analysis and participation roadmap
  • Comment drafting for regulatory consultations
  • Working group participation strategy
  • Industry coalition positioning advice
